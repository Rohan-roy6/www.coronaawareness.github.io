What is COVID-19 ?

Full Form: Corona Virus Disease(2019)
It was spread from the city of Wuhan in China.The Covid-19 infection is caused by a member of the coronavirus family that had never been encountered before. Most probably, it had transmitted to human from animals. The World Health Organization (WHO) has declared it a pandemic.

Symptoms of Covid-19

According to the W.H.O, the most common symptoms of Covid-19 are fever,dry cough and tiredness. Some patients may also have a runny nose, sore throat.
